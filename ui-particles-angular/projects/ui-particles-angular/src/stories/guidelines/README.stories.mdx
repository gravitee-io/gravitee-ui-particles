# Guidelines

These guidelines are for everyone who is writing in products interfaces.

## Tone

Tone describes how the Gravitee voice is expressed.

- Gravity's tone should be clear, informed, professional and relevant

## Best practices

### Title

- The main title of a page is placed above all the cards on the page.
- Secondary titles can be optional if the context in the cart is explicit.
- Secondary titles are placed at the top of each card.

### Tend to avoid abbreviations

We try to avoid abbreviations that are difficult to understand and random to improve understanding of our products.
